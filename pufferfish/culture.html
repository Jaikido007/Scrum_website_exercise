Popular Culture!

Who doesn't love a big puffy spikey fish?

The pufferfish has been represented in a number of popular shows in the past few decades. From Qwilfish the Pokemon which respresents a pufferfish to Skyes from Shark Tale and Bloat from Finding Nemo.

Whilst the characters are usually presented in a positive light as sidekicks to the main protagonists they are also portrayed as fearful characters that blow up easily when scared or nervous. Yet this has not translated to having any serious impacts in the real world.
